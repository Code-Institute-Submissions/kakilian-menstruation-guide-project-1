What is menstruation?

Firstly, it is normal vaginal bleeding, that occurs in cycles, From 21 to 45 days in earlier years From 21 to 31 days in young adult lives, and right up until age 51.

Is can start as young as 10 years old also up to 15 years of age. All ages are normal. It depends on the individual body, and diet.

“Menstrual blood and tissue flow from your uterus through a small opening in your cervix and pass out of your body, though your Vagina. The uterus lining builds up to prepare for pregnancy” Quelle “ Womans Health”

Symptoms?

Common symptoms, know as PMS – Premenstinal syndrome;

Breast tenderness, bloating, headaches, mood swings, depression, anxiety, anger and irritability, lower backpains and also diahorre.

House wife tales

Myth 1: Will I faint during my PMS, period? No, the blood loss is minimal, even tho it will seem there is a large amount of blood loss.

Myth 2: what? -No sports? Of course you can still do sports. You should listen to your body. Sports help the flow of the bleeding, and can relieve pain for some woman.

Myth 3: Don’t wash your hair? Oh, dear, have to be one of my favorite nonsense ones. Of course wash your hair, hygiene - daily showers, baths – keep yourself clean, when possible bath, keeps you fresh and will help relieve pains, symptoms,. Or and mood.

Myth 4: Will people know? You wont have a “I have my PMS written anywhere on your body” so no. Having your period isn’t a dirty secret. You should never feel like, “I’ve done something wrong” or “I’m not normal”. It’s a completely normal body process beginning early on in younger years.
